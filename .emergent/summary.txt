<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user wants to build a premium, fast-loading, single-page, mobile-responsive PWA named NBA 2K Legacy Vault. The site's purpose is to serve as a concept pitch to bring back older NBA 2K games (2K15, 2K16, 2K17, 2K20) with persistent online servers.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Aesthetic:** Strict red (#C8102E), black (#000000), and white (#FFFFFF) color palette. It should feel like a premium mobile app with smooth scroll animations and a clean look.
- **PWA:** Must be fully installable on mobile devices.
- **Admin Capabilities:** The user must have full administrative control to manage all content on the site. This includes:
    - **Games:** Add, edit, delete, reorder, and toggle visibility of games.
    - **Site Text:** Edit all headlines, taglines, and descriptive text, including the main Legacy Vault concept pitch.
    - **Media:** Add unlimited video clips (YouTube, TikTok, etc.) to each game.
    - **Community Engagement:** Reply to comments with a special ADMIN badge, view/delete comments, and see comment likes.
    - **Petition:** Manage a petition by viewing signatures and adding them in bulk to show social proof.
    - **Subscribers:** View and export a list of users who sign up for email notifications.
    - **Proof of Demand:** Upload screenshots/images with descriptions to showcase community demand for the concept.
- **Key Sections:**
    - **Hero:** A full-screen section with a headline, tagline, and call-to-action buttons.
    - **The Games:** A grid of game cards. Clicking a card opens a modal with details and associated video clips.
    - **The Legacy Vault:** A section detailing the concept, featuring a multi-game cover banner, and a link to a full Google Doc concept.
    - **Community:** A live comment section (with replies and likes), an email signup form, and a petition counter.
- **Admin Panel:** A password-protected  route that provides access to all the management features listed above.
